As a computational biologist, I often write my own code to carry out my research, being most proficient in Python, JavaScript, and Bash. I am a strong supporter of open science and open-source software, and I pride myself in writing readable and reproducible code. As a result, nearly all of my contributions are available on my [github page](https://github.com/joaorodrigues) under permissive licenses.

Over the years, I authored several open-source packages for bioinformatics that I am particularly proud of, such as [pdb-tools](https://haddocking.org/pdb-tools) and [fcc](https://github.com/haddocking/fcc). I also help maintain the *Bio.PDB* module of [Biopython](https://biopython.org). More recently, I started looking into using JavaScript for structural biology and developed [nmaJS](https://joaorodrigues.github.io/nmaJS).
