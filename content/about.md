I am a computational biologist and scientific software developer interested in protein structure and dynamics. I studied Biochemistry at the University of Coimbra, in Portugal, and later learned about structural biology and bioinformatics in the labs of [Alexandre Bonvin](https://bonvinlab.org) in Utrecht and [Michael Levitt](https://med.stanford.edu/levitt.html) at Stanford. 

My work has resulted in several [peer-reviewed publications](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=UeXRdzQAAAAJ&hl=en-US) and some useful software packages for [structural bioinformatics](https://github.com/JoaoRodrigues). In particular, I am the author and lead developer of [pdb-tools](https://haddocking.org/pdb-tools) and of the [fcc clustering](https://github.com/haddocking/fcc) packages, and I help maintain the *Bio.PDB* module of [Biopython](https://biopython.org). Recently, I have also started looking into using JavaScript for structural biology and developed [nmaJS](https://joaorodrigues.github.io/nmaJS).

In addition to research, I am enthusiastic about community-building, teaching, and mentoring, I am a certified [Software Carpentry](https://software-carpentry.org/) instructor, and I have developed few [tutorials of my own](/education).
