I am a computational biologist interested in protein structure, dynamics, and interactions. I studied Biochemistry at the University of Coimbra, in Portugal, and later learned about structural biology and bioinformatics in the labs of [Alexandre Bonvin](https://bonvinlab.org) in Utrecht and [Michael Levitt](https://med.stanford.edu/levitt.html) at Stanford. 

My work has resulted in several [peer-reviewed publications](/science) and some useful software packages for [structural bioinformatics](/code). In addition to research, I am enthusiastic about community-building and [teaching](/teaching), having helped organize multiple conferences and courses and being a certified [Software Carpentry](https://software-carpentry.org/) instructor.
